Hi! My name is Alex and I am a researcher at OpenAI working on image generation.

Until recently, I was the co-founder and CTO at Luma AI, where I worked on generative models, especially dream-machine, a frontier video generative model, and genie, a text-to-3d model.

Previously, I graduated from UC Berkeley, where I majored in CS and applied math and worked with Prof. Angjoo Kanazawa in BAIR. While at Berkeley, I also interned at Google and Adobe, helped search for aliens at SETI, particiated in programming competitions, and released various open source projects on Github, among other things. Before Berkeley, I lived in Vancouver, Canada for many years.
