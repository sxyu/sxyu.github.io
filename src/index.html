About

Hi! My name is Alex and I am a recent graduate from UC Berkeley, where I majored in CS and applied math. I'll be a founding research scientist at Luma AI starting Spring 2022, and am also planning to start a PhD in Fall 2022. Until the end of winter, I'm working on 3D computer vision research with {{ autolink("Prof. Angjoo Kanazawa") }} in BAIR where I worked as a undergrad researcher. My research interest is at the intersection of vision and graphics. Recently, I am particularly interested in differentiable rendering and neural representations.

During my undergrad, I also dabbled in CS theory, and worked on research in query complexity with Prof. Avishay Tal, without much success. Due to this I took many theory-related graduate classes, and was was also a member of the undergrad CS Theory club (UGTCS).

Prior to Berkeley, I lived in Vancouver, Canada for many years. In fact, for my junior and senior years, I was based in the Vancouver area with my family and studying remotely.
